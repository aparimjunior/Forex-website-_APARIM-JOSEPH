Trading Strategies

Day Trading

Open and close positions within the same trading day to capitalize on short-term movements.

Swing Trading

Hold positions for several days to capture medium-term price movements.

Position Trading

Long-term approach focusing on fundamental factors that drive currency values.

Algorithmic Trading

Using automated systems to execute trades based on predefined criteria.

Market Analysis

Technical Analysis

Study price charts and use indicators to identify patterns and predict future movements:

  • Support & Resistance levels
  • Trend lines and channels
  • Candlestick patterns
  • Moving averages, RSI, MACD, etc.
  • Fibonacci retracements

Fundamental Analysis

Analyze economic indicators and news events that affect currency values:

  • Interest rates and central bank policies
  • Economic growth (GDP)
  • Employment data
  • Inflation indicators
  • Political stability

Sentiment Analysis

Gauge market psychology and positioning:

  • COT (Commitment of Traders) reports
  • Risk appetite indicators
  • News sentiment analysis
  • Positioning ratios
